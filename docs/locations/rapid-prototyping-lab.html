Rapid Prototyping Lab

Located in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, within the School of Engineering and Applied Science, the RPL is a state-of-the-art polymer additive manufacturing facility that provides Computer-Aided Design, and FDM and Poly-Jet 3D Printing services to our students, faculty, staff, and external clients. We welcome sponsored research projects and grant collaborations. We have three Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) 3D printers that use ASA plastic. They have a 10” x 10” x 10” build platform, and a 0.01 inch layer resolution. We have one Poly-Jet 3D printer. With material properties from rigid to flexible, 14 different materials can be printed in the same part. Out poly-jet machine has a 18” x 14” x 6” build platform, and 30 micron layer resolution.

http://rpl.mae.virginia.edu/
