Scholars' Lab Makerspace

The Scholars' Lab Makerspace is a place for tinkering and experimentation with technologies like desktop fabrication, physical computing, and augmented reality. Open to everyone, we specialize in applications and research questions in the humanities and arts. Interested in exploring the Makerspace? Have an idea to use microcontrollers or 3D modeling and printing technology to enhance your research or differently interrogate your assumptions? Contact us at slabmakerspace@virginia.edu to schedule an appointment with Scholars' Lab faculty and staff to discuss your planned project.

http://scholarslab.org/makerspace
