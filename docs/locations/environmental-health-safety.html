Environmental Health & Safety

Environmental Health &Safety (EHS) provides and coordinates services for the University's Makerspace, Shop and Studio Managers. You can find safety and health resources at our EHS website including: the University Policy for students working safely in these environments; the User Agreement to use tools and equipment in University Maker Lab Spaces, Shops, and Studios; and Online Safety Training Modules. Please give us a call,we love "a day in the shop"!
