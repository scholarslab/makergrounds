A-School Fablab

http://www.arch.virginia.edu/fabrication

There are three main facilities: the Woodshop with the Victor Elmaleh Fabrications Lab, the CNC Lab, and the Milton Airport Facility. Students go through a mandatory training when they enter the School of Architecture, and we always welcome UVa students, faculty, and staff outside of the School to get trained to use the facilities.

Woodshop

The Woodshop is fully equipped with tools for traditional or analog woodworking. The Shops also have a fleet of sewing machines, including a Bernina 830 Embroidery Machine for CNC Sewing.

3D Printers

Stratasys Dimension ABS 3D Printer, 3D Digitizer and 3D Laser Scanner.

CNC Lab

The CNC Lab is host to a 5'x10' Onsrud 3 Axis CNC Router, the Techno 3 Axis CNC Router, two Universal Laser Cutters, and a Denford MicroMill 2000.
