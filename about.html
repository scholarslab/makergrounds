Maker Grounds @ UVA

A site for finding resources and learning about Makerspaces, Shops, Labs, and other spaces for making at the University of Virginia.

Website

This website is built and maintained by Ammon Shepherd, UVA Library Scholars' Lab, and Jason Bennett, Wilson Hall Maker Studio.

You can send any questions or comments by sending an email to makergroundwebsite@virginia.edu
